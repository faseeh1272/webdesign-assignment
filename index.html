His influence extends to MK Jewelry (China), MK Smoke & Novelties, and Green Gems Investment Ltd. With an unwavering commitment to growth, Moiz Khoja continues to expand his impact across industries, setting new standards for innovation and success worldwide.

Moiz Khoja
